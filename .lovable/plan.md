

# Haven Chavous - Personal Portfolio Site

A polished, dark-themed multi-page portfolio website inspired by Quanthaven Labs' aesthetic — featuring deep dark backgrounds with subtle purple/violet gradient accents, clean modern typography, and smooth animations.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section**: Bold introduction with your name, professional tagline ("Financial Modeling Innovator | AI-Enhanced Analytics | Excel Guru"), and a call-to-action to explore your work
- **Quick highlights**: 15+ years experience, key accomplishments, and links to main sections
- **Subtle animated gradient background** matching the Quanthaven Labs style

### 2. About Page
- **Professional Summary**: Your story bridging traditional financial modeling with modern AI solutions
- **Career Philosophy**: What drives your work at the intersection of quantitative finance and AI
- **Education**: MBA in Finance (University of Delaware), BBA in Finance (Augusta State University)

### 3. Experience Page
- **Interactive Timeline**: Visual career progression from DuPont through Hearthfire Holdings
- **Role Cards**: Each position with company, dates, and key achievements
- **Highlights**: Focus on major accomplishments like the $1B+ portfolio acquisitions, 90% report automation, and web application transformations

### 4. Projects Page
- **Portfolio Showcase**: Featured work with descriptions
  - Hearthfire Analytics Portal & AI Document Management Platform
  - Investment Returns Modeling Platform
  - Python Data Transformation Pipelines
  - Consulting highlights (Source Renewables, Harvard Business School, VisualDx, CDW)
- **Card-based grid layout** with hover effects

### 5. Skills Page
- **Technical Skills Grid**: Python, SQL, JavaScript/HTML, Excel (Expert)
- **Core Competencies**: Financial Modeling, Investment Analysis, AI Integration, Quantitative Methods
- **Visual skill indicators** organized by category

### 6. Contact Page
- **Contact Form**: Name, email, message fields (will submit via email link since static)
- **Direct Contact Info**: Email and phone
- **Location**: Wilmington, DE
- **Professional links** section (for future LinkedIn or GitHub if desired)

---

## Design Details

- **Dark Theme**: Deep charcoal/navy backgrounds with subtle purple/violet gradient accents
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif fonts with excellent readability
- **Navigation**: Persistent header with smooth page transitions
- **Responsive**: Optimized for desktop and mobile viewing
- **Animations**: Subtle hover effects, scroll animations, and gradient transitions
